# business-lab

Visual business management. 10 self-contained demos, one per idea from the IE BCSAI *Introduction to Business Management* course (Jorge Dinarés).

**Live:** https://andreaisabelmontana.github.io/business-lab/

Module 1 — Foundations of business
1. Supply & demand — drag the curves, find equilibrium price/quantity, read surplus vs shortage at any price line

Module 2 — Strategic thinking & tools
2. Price elasticity of demand — slide price along a linear demand curve; elastic / inelastic regions and the revenue-maximising point at `|E| = 1`
4. Cost curves — U-shaped average cost, rising marginal cost, profit-maximising output where `MC = MR`
5. Product life cycle — logistic adoption S-curve with new-adopters bell; introduction → growth → maturity → decline
6. BCG growth-share matrix — drag business units across Stars / Cash Cows / Question Marks / Dogs
8. Porter's five forces — rate each force on a pentagon radar; mean pressure → industry attractiveness

Module 3 — Organizational design & leadership
7. Weighted decision matrix — score options against weighted criteria; click cells to adjust, watch the winner change
9. Organizational structure — balanced hierarchy by span of control; layers, managers, flat vs tall shape

Module 4 — Functional areas (finance)
3. Break-even analysis — fixed/variable cost + price → break-even units, profit and loss regions shaded

Module 5 — Digital transformation & scaling
10. SaaS growth — MRR compounding with churn; steady state `M* = new / churn`, growing vs shrinking trajectory

Plain HTML + canvas + KaTeX. Indigo accent. Zero build step.
